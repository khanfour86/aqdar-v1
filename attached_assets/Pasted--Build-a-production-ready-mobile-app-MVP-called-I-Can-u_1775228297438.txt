**Build a production-ready mobile app MVP called “أنا أقدر” (I Can) using React Native with Expo and a scalable architecture prepared for future integrations.**

The app concept is a habit-breaking and self-improvement application focused on helping Arabic-speaking users quit harmful habits such as smoking, vaping, digital addiction, and other custom habits.

---

## Core Product Vision

The app should feel modern, motivational, emotionally supportive, and highly intuitive.

The design language should be:

* clean
* premium
* minimal
* psychologically calming
* modern Arabic-first UI

Use a professional color palette that matches the idea of self-control, healing, progress, and achievement.

Suggested color direction:

* calming dark navy / deep blue as base
* supportive green for progress
* gold / soft amber for trophies and achievements
* white / light gray cards
* subtle gradients for hero sections

The branding should feel elegant and simple.

---

## App Branding

The app name must be displayed as:

**أنا أقدر**

Use a clean Arabic typography style.

The app logo should be text-based and minimal.

Make the app name visually strong and premium.

Under the app name on the splash / onboarding screen, create animated rotating phrases such as:

* أوقف التدخين
* أترك الفيب
* أتحكم بنفسي
* أبدأ من جديد
* أقدر أكمل

These phrases should animate smoothly under the main title.

Example behavior:
The phrase fades/slides out and another phrase fades/slides in.

Keep the animation elegant and modern.

## Screens to Build

### 1. Splash / Welcome Screen

* centered app name “أنا أقدر”
* animated supporting phrases underneath
* modern CTA buttons
* login options:

  * Apple
  * Google
  * Facebook
  * Guest mode

---

### 2. Profile Setup Screen

Required fields:

* nickname (required)
* age (required)
* currency (required)

Optional:

* country
* gender

Currency must be stored at account level in settings, not per habit.

---

### 3. Habit Selection Screen

Beautiful icon-based selection cards for:

* Smoking
* Vape
* Social media
* Digital harmful content
* Caffeine
* Custom habit

Free version should allow one habit only.

Architecture must support multiple habits later.

---

### 4. Habit Setup Screen

For smoking example:

* packs per day
* daily cost
* quit mode:

  * immediate
  * gradual

The architecture must support reusable habit configuration templates for all future habits.

---

### 5. Main Dashboard

Must include:

* live streak timer
* days / hours
* total money saved
* avoided count
* current trophy
* progress ring
* motivational AI message card
* craving button
* relapse button

Important buttons:

* “أشعر برغبة الآن”
* “تعثرت اليوم”

---

### 6. Trophy / Achievement System

Design and implement a scalable trophy system.

Create visually beautiful trophy cards and icons.

Examples:

* بداية جديدة
* أول 24 ساعة
* 3 أيام
* أسبوع
* وفرت 10 د.ك
* أول شهر

The trophy system must be extensible for premium achievements.

Please design the trophy visuals professionally.
